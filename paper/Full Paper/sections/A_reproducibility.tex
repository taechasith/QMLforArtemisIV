\section*{Appendix: Reproducibility and Evidence Ledger}

Each main-text number can be traced to a copied source table or figure. The
Gate 5 endpoint and successor-branch CSV files in \texttt{results\_tables/}
supply the reported values; the figure registry records each included figure's
source data, generator, digest, and claim boundary. The release also provides
scripts, fixed configuration records, data cards, and decision logs.
Reproduction starts from the tagged repository state and preserves the
calibration and final-test lock; rerunning a report script is not authorization
for fitting or a new campaign.

\subsection*{Interpretation Checklist for Reviewers}

Read Gate 3 as eligible-scope simulator consistency, not mission validation;
Gate 5 as rejection of Q01 on this development benchmark, not of all QML; and
Q01b, FQK, and D034--D049 as development-only evidence that cannot revise Gate
5 or open Gate 6. D046 shows why an apparent C06 improvement also needs a
like-for-like classical control. The recall-first record is a future
protocol-design lesson, not a safety filter. No figure or table supports
quantum advantage, hardware performance, propellant saving, or flight
readiness.
