\subsection*{Literature-Source Audit}

The literature review was re-audited against the completed experiment. Table
\ref{tab:literature-audit} accounts for the 23 accepted records in the
repository extraction matrix, plus the random-Fourier source that underpins the
implemented A01 control and the quantum-kernel benchmark source retained for
the P001 discussion. The full extraction matrix remains the provenance record;
the bibliography contains only sources that support an explicit statement,
design choice, control, or scope decision in this manuscript.

The audit does not convert prior literature into evidence for the present
models. In particular, variational QML, quantum reinforcement learning, and
quantum annealing sources are cited to explain why a model family was frozen,
controlled, or excluded. The empirical findings remain those in the registered
public-data development campaigns described in this paper.
