# Release Simulator Card

Version: 0.1.0
Decision: D028-C
Prepared: 2026-07-14
Status: Release-support card; research simulator evidence only

## Simulator Scope

The simulator supports public-data research benchmarking for cislunar
trajectory-correction planning. It is not a flight dynamics product and is not
approved for operational use.

## Credibility Evidence

Gate 3 established simulator credibility for the eligible validation evidence.
RTC3 remains outside the validation claim because eligible evidence was not
available under the frozen rule.

## Intended Use

Use the simulator record to understand how the benchmark generated and checked
research evidence under public-data and reference-laptop constraints.

## Prohibited Use

Do not interpret simulator results as flight readiness, NASA approval,
mission-owned validation, or astronaut-use readiness.

## Release Boundary

D028-C authorizes no mission-loop experiment, Gate 6 run, locked-data access,
hardware/GPU execution, or operational claim.
