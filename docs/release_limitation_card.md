# Release Limitation Card

Version: 0.1.0
Decision: D028-C
Prepared: 2026-07-14
Status: Release-support card; required limitations before release decision

## Primary Limitations

- The strongest defensible result is a negative QML benchmark under frozen
  public-data development conditions.
- No QML Gate 6 mission candidate is eligible from P001.
- Calibration, final-test, mission-loop, hardware/GPU, and Gate 6 evidence
  remain unavailable.
- The reference laptop influenced compute-admission design and should not be
  treated as a required future research platform.
- Recall-first CSAFE-RF is a future safety-objective lesson, not an advancing
  model result.

## Prohibited Conclusions

The release must not claim quantum advantage, fuel savings, flight readiness,
NASA approval, operational Orion relevance, Gate 6 authorization, or a rescue
of Gate 5.

## Next Release Requirement

Before any public release decision, the project still needs a clean
reproducibility audit, release checklist, claim review, and human acceptance of
the final manuscript/release package.
