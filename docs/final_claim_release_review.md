## Scope

D031-C reviews the release package after D026-C claim synthesis, D027-C
manuscript drafting, D028-C release cards, and D030-C clean reproducibility
PASS. It is a decision-preparation record only.

## Allowed Release Candidate Claim

A public-data, development-only benchmark found no tested QML candidate that
outperformed strong classical controls or qualified for a QML Gate 6 mission
experiment.

This claim must be labeled as development-only benchmark evidence. It is not
final-test, calibration, mission-loop, operational, NASA, flight-readiness, or
quantum-advantage evidence.

## Required Labels

- Development-only benchmark.
- Negative QML result.
- No QML Gate 6 candidate from P001.
- Calibration and final-test remain locked.
- Release requires explicit human acceptance.

## Prohibited Claims

- QML improves cislunar propellant efficiency.
- Quantum advantage was observed.
- Mission-loop validation was completed.
- Flight readiness or NASA approval.
- Gate 6 is authorized.
- A new QML invention has been proven.

## Human Decision Required

The human research lead must choose one of:

- Accept release package.
- Request revisions.
- Reject release.

D031-C authorizes no release, tag, archive, DOI, Gate 6 run, locked-data
access, mission-loop execution, model fitting, QML invention claim, or
quantum-advantage claim. RFIG-050 records this decision point.
